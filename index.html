What is it: Specialized deep learning architecture designed for processing grid-like data such as images, utilizing convolutional layers to automatically learn spatial hierarchies of features.

Key concepts: Convolutional Layers, Pooling, Feature Maps, Receptive Field, Filters/Kernels, Transfer Learning, Data Augmentation

Real-life usage: Computer vision applications including image classification, object detection, facial recognition, medical imaging analysis, autonomous driving, and video analysis.
